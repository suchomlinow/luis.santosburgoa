About Me

I am currently pursuing an M.S. in Computational Analysis and Public Policy at the Harris School of Public Policy at the University of Chicago. I hold a B.A. in Industrial Engineering from ITAM in Mexico, and I have more than five years of experience working in the Economic Research Department at the Bank of Mexico as a Specialty Leader Analyst.

I am interested in Institutions that make a tangible impact on people's lives. I am a firm believer in the efficacy of local solutions to impact global scales. Let´s connect!
